Art Mate: What to Draw? Support

Welcome to the support page for Art Mate: What to Draw? Here you can find answers to common questions and contact information for further assistance.

Common Issues and FAQs

Q: Can I draw inside the app?

A: No, Art Mate is not a drawing app. It provides inspiration and challenges to help you decide what to draw. You can use any drawing tools you prefer outside the app.

Q: How does the emojinator slot machine work?

A: The emojinator slot machine generates random combinations of emojis to spark your creativity. Interpret these combinations in any way you like to start your drawing.

Q: Can I share my drawings with the community?

A: While you can't share drawings directly through the app, you can share them on Instagram and tag our account @art_mate_space.

Q: What are the challenges in Art Mate?

A: Challenges in Art Mate are handpicked sets of emojis and references based on specific topics. Each challenge has a time limit. These challenges help you explore new themes and push your creativity.
